Testing

Website Testing Services

At Amper Digital, we specialize in comprehensive website testing services designed for legal standards. Whether you require one-time testing or ongoing assessments, we have the expertise to ensure your digital presence is accessible to all.

Comprehensive WCAG audit reports

Our tests cover 10 to 20 pages on your website, allowing us to provide a granular breakdown of each Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 standard, detailing pass/fail results. You'll receive a comprehensive report outlining areas for improvement and immediate action.

Our testing process includes:

  • Manual testing: Our experienced accessibility engineers conduct thorough manual assessments. Their in-depth knowledge of accessibility regulations ensures a meticulous examination.
  • Usability testing: We work with disabled users to ensure that your website is not only compliant but also user-friendly for all.
  • Screen reader testing: We test with popular screen reader software like JAWS and NVDA to guarantee compatibility and usability.
  • Automated testing: We employ automated testing software to identify potential issues and violations of accessibility standards.

Annual testing

For public sector bodies, we highly recommend annual testing to maintain compliance with the Public Sector Body Accessibility Regulations (PSBAR). Regular evaluations help you maintain compliance and identify issues early.

Beyond websites

In addition to website testing, we offer assessment and remediation for other digital assets, including PDFs to meet the PDF/UA standard. Our ad-hoc remediation service ensures that all your documents conform to required standards.

With Amper Digital, you can be confident in the accessibility and compliance of your digital content. We're here to make the digital world more accessible, one assessment at a time.
